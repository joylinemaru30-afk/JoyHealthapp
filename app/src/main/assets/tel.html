Emergency Helpline

Toll-Free Emergency Number

Call us anytime for immediate assistance and health support.

1-800-555-0100
Available 24 Hours a Day, 7 Days a Week

When to Call This Number

  • Medical emergencies such as severe injury, heart attack, or difficulty breathing.
  • Fire, explosion, or gas leak situations.
  • Criminal activity, violence, or personal safety threats.
  • Accidents on roads, highways, or public areas.
  • Natural disasters or hazardous environmental conditions.

If you’re unsure whether your situation is an emergency, it’s always safer to call.

Before You Call

  • Stay calm and speak clearly.
  • Provide your name, phone number, and exact location.
  • Describe the situation briefly and accurately.
  • Follow the operator’s instructions carefully.

Additional Services

  • Multilingual Support: Assistance available in English, Spanish, and regional languages.
  • Hearing Impaired Support: Text and TTY communication options available.
  • Confidential Assistance: All calls are handled with privacy and care.
  • Health & Safety Guidance: Non-emergency health information and advice.
