Typhoid Symptoms

Introduction

Typhoid fever is a bacterial infection caused by Salmonella Typhi. It spreads through contaminated food and water and can become life-threatening if not treated. Recognizing the symptoms early is crucial for proper diagnosis and treatment.

Main Symptoms of Typhoid
