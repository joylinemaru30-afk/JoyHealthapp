About Waterborne Disease: Typhoid

Waterborne diseases are illnesses caused by dirty or contaminated water or an unclean environment. They spread easily and have affected many areas of the world. There are many types of waterborne diseases, but we will focus on one of the most widespread and dangerous â€” Typhoid.

Where it was First Discovered?

This disease has existed for thousands of years and was known as far back as Ancient Greece.

Infected People

The disease has infected a large portion of the population in various regions of the world.

How it Spreads

Typhoid spreads through improper cleaning of food, poor sanitation, and lack of good hygiene.
